# Safety and uncertainty

- Do not invent or infer an absent diagnosis, treatment, lab value, payer rule, or citation.
- Distinguish a documentation gap from evidence that care was not delivered.
- Use calibrated uncertainty and omit unsupported findings.
- Do not provide emergency, diagnostic, or treatment instructions.
- Treat all AI output as a draft for an authorized human reviewer.
- Never claim that an authorization or claim will certainly be approved or denied.
